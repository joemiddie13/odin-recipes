Traeger Ranger Pizza

Make a deep dish pizza in your Traeger Ranger!

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil
  • Pizza Dough: 10 oz.
  • Pizza Sauce: 1/2 Cup
  • Pepperonis: LOTS
  • Sausage of your choice: PLENTY
  • Spinach: SOME
  • Mozzarella Cheese: LOTS
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Red Peppers
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. When ready to cook, set the Traeger temperature to 450˚F and preheat with the lid closed for 15 minutes. For optimal results, set to 450° if available.
  2. Coat a 10-inch or 12-inch cast iron pan with olive oil. Add the dough to the pan and press out through the bottom and up around the sides.
  3. Assemble the pizza. Spread the sauce on top of the dough, and add the sausage, pepperoni, and spinach. Add mozzarella and freshly grated parmesan on top, and sprinkle with oregano and basil.
  4. Place the cast iron directly on the grill grates, close the lid, and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and cheese and sauce are bubbling.
  5. Remove pizza and let it kick-back for about 10 minutes before slicing!
